Panda Panda Panda

Panda Panda Panda was a game created by my partner Lina and I. The purpose of this game was to have
the panda collect as much bamboo as possible to add onto his bowl of ramen. If he does not get bamboo, he
cannot eat his ramen in peace. In order to create this game,we solely based our game on the if-then variables
and motion variables such as using the x and y position to correctly position the panda on the screen
and enabling it to move around. For me, the most challenging part about creating the game was
being able to change backgrounds and enabling the panda to do its own script even after the backdrop switches.
I also had problems with making the loops and making the panda glide across the screen.
